On the other hand, metallic oxide electrodes are created using metal oxide materials like indium tin oxide (ITO) or zinc oxide. These different types provide varying degrees of transparency, electrical conductivity, and flexibility, making them suitable for different applications in industries like electronics, solar cells, displays, and touchscreens.</p></p>
