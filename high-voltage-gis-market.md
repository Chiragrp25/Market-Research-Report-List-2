<p><p>The high voltage GIS (gas-insulated switchgear) market encompasses various types of switchgear systems. Isolated Phase GIS refers to a type where each phase is enclosed in a separate metal enclosure, ensuring complete isolation. Integrated 3 Phase GIS combines all three phases into a single metal enclosure, reducing both the space required and installation costs. Hybrid GIS employs a combination of air-insulated and SF6 gas-insulated components, providing a more flexible and economical solution. Lastly, the "Other" market refers to any unique or specialized GIS systems that may not fit into the aforementioned categories, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
