<p><p>The fiber optic jumper market is categorized into two types: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode jumpers use a single transmission mode, allowing for long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. They are widely used in telecommunications and long-distance networking applications. On the other hand, multimode jumpers facilitate shorter distance communication and are commonly used within buildings or campuses. They support multiple transmission modes, enabling efficient data transmission through a larger core size. The choice between single-mode and multimode jumpers depends on the required transmission distance and application environment.</p></p>
