<p><p>Degradable plastic bags market includes four main types: Polylactic acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), Starch Blends, and Others. PLA bags are made from renewable resources like corn or sugar cane, and they break down into natural elements in composting conditions. PHA bags are produced from microorganisms and can be composted in industrial facilities. Starch blend bags are made by blending starch and other materials, offering enhanced biodegradability. The "Others" category encompasses various degradable plastic bag alternatives, including oxo-degradable and bio-based plastics, which break down under certain environmental conditions.</p></p>
