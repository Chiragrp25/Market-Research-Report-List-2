<p><p>The Wafer CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) Pads market consists of two main types: Hard CMP Pads and Soft CMP Pads. Hard CMP Pads are designed with a rigid material to provide high removal rates and excellent planarization control for wafer manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Soft CMP Pads are made from a more flexible material that offers improved defect reduction and surface finishing capabilities. Both types of pads play crucial roles in achieving precise polishing results for the semiconductor industry, each with their unique benefits and applications.</p></p>
