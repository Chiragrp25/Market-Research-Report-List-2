<p><p>Image sensor chips are a key component in digital cameras and other imaging devices. CCD (charge-coupled device) and CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor) are the two primary types of image sensor technologies in the market. CCD sensors use a complex manufacturing process that provides high-quality images but consume more power. In contrast, CMOS sensors, considered more advanced, use less power, have faster processing capabilities and offer better integration with other functions. Both types have their advantages and are used in various applications, depending on the specific requirements of the devices.</p></p>
